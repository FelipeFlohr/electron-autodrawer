Coordinates

The Autodrawer will use these coordinates to interact with Paint 3D UI. Every coordinate is based on your cursor XY coordinate within your screen space.
Load default values?
Default values are based on a 1920x1080 resolution and a 4000x3000 canvas. It might not be compatible if your resolution is different. If you load default values, every data will be overwritten.
